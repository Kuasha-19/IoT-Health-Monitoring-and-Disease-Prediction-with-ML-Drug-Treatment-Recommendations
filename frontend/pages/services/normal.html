Preventive Healthcare

General Health Screening

Holistic analysis of baseline physiological metrics.

Diagnostic Scope

The General Health Screening serves as a comprehensive baseline evaluation. It cross-references all vital signs—temperature, blood pressure, heart rate, and humidity—against standard healthy benchmarks to ensure homeostasis and rule out underlying acute conditions.

  • Homeostasis Verification
  • Baseline Vital Sign Recording
  • Preventive Risk Factor Identification

Institutional Guidance

Routine screenings are recommended every 6 months to maintain accurate clinical history and early detection capabilities.
