Vascular Medicine

Hypertension Assessment

Systematic screening for elevated arterial blood pressure levels.

Diagnostic Scope

Hypertension evaluation involves the analysis of high-pressure load on arterial walls. The system categorizes readings into Elevated, Stage 1, or Stage 2 Hypertension, assessing the risk of secondary complications like renal strain or cerebrovascular events.

  • Systolic Pressure Load Calculation
  • Vascular Resistance Estimation
  • Chronic Load Pattern Recognition

Medical Alert

Hypertension is often asymptomatic. Regular screening is essential for the prevention of sudden cardiovascular failure.
