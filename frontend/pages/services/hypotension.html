Internal Medicine

Hypotension Assessment

Detection of low blood pressure and systemic perfusion risks.

Diagnostic Scope

This assessment identifies abnormally low blood pressure levels that may compromise oxygen delivery to vital organs. It screens for symptoms such as syncope, vertigo, and blurred vision to determine if the hypotension is chronic or acute.

  • Diastolic Minimum Threshold Tracking
  • Perfusion Pressure Analysis
  • Orthostatic Variance Mapping

Medical Alert

Immediate intervention is required if low blood pressure is accompanied by confusion, cold/clammy skin, or rapid, shallow breathing (Shock).
