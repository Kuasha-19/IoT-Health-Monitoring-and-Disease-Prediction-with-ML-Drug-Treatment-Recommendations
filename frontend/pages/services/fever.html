Fever and Respiratory Analysis

Evaluation of thermoregulatory response and pulmonary function markers.

Diagnostic Scope

This module analyzes febrile patterns in conjunction with respiratory symptoms. By correlating body temperature fluctuations with cough, fatigue, and shortness of breath, the AI identifies potential viral or bacterial pulmonary infections, including pneumonia and bronchitis.

  • Core Temperature Variance Analysis
  • Respiratory Rate Consistency
  • Symptom Progression Mapping
  • Viral vs Bacterial Indicators

Medical Alert

Seek urgent care if you experience a temperature above 39.4°C (103°F) or significant difficulty breathing.
