Diagnostic Scope

The General Health Screening serves as a comprehensive baseline evaluation. It cross-references all vital signs—including temperature, blood pressure, heart rate, and environmental humidity—against standard healthy benchmarks to ensure systemic homeostasis and rule out underlying acute conditions.
